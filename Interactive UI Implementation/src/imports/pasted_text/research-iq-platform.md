	SYSTEM NAME : RESEARCH IQ

AI-Based Research Intelligence Platform for Academic Collaboration

System Overview

Academic collaboration and research productivity increasingly depend on effective discovery of research expertise, ongoing projects, funding opportunities, and institutional research outputs. In many universities, research information is fragmented across departments, repositories, and individual researcher profiles, making it difficult to identify collaboration opportunities and research synergies.

This project proposes the design and development of an AI-Based Research Intelligence Platform that applies artificial intelligence and data analytics techniques to analyze research publications, projects, researcher profiles, and thematic areas in order to support intelligent academic collaboration. The system will provide expertise mapping, collaboration recommendations, research trend analysis, and analytics dashboards that support institutional research planning and collaborative innovation.

The platform is designed to complement existing institutional repositories and research management systems by providing intelligent analytics and collaboration intelligence rather than replacing operational academic systems. The implementation will focus on a prototype suitable for academic evaluation and institutional demonstration.

Objectives

General Objective

To design and develop an AI-driven research intelligence platform that enhances academic collaboration through research analytics, expertise mapping, and intelligent recommendation mechanisms.

Specific Objectives

To integrate research publications, projects, and researcher profile data into a unified platform

To develop AI models for expertise identification and collaboration recommendation

To analyze research trends and thematic research strengths

To provide dashboards supporting research planning and collaboration decisions

To generate reports supporting institutional research strategy and funding alignment
